About AMCARES

AMCARES (Awareness and Medical Care for Organ Recipients and Supporters) is a non-profit dedicated to promoting organ donation in India. Our mission is to educate communities, facilitate organ pledges, and support patients and families through the transplant journey. With only 0.65 donors per million population in India, we aim to bridge the gap between the 500,000 organs needed annually and the 3,500 transplants performed.
